Here are 10 hair hack tips!

  • Do not wash your hair everyday because shampoo traps your natural oils, which dries your hair out, unless you have really oily hair; wash it more frequently.
  • After you shampoo your hair, leave your conditioner in for a couple minutes so it can moisturize and soften your hair.
  • The less heat you give to your hair the better.
  • Using cold water strengthens and shines the hair.
  • Use a t-shirt to wrap your hair instead of a towel.
  • Do not brush your hair when it is wet, it can cause breakage to it.
  • About every 7 weeks, trim your hair one fourth of an inch to avoid the split ends.
  • Using coconut, almond, castor, and olive oil for your hair is amazing.
  • Smoking and eating unhealthy is bad for your hair.
  • Clean out your brushes!!! Do not use the same ones forever.
