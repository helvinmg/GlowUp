Hair Tips

Here are 10 hair hack tips!


  1. Don't wash your hair everyday, shampoo traps your natural oils, which dries your hair out.
  2. Leave your conditioner in for a couple minutes so it can moisturize and soften your hair.
  3. The less heat you give to your hair the better.
  4. Using cold water strengthens and shines the hair.
  5. Use a t-shirt to wrap your hair instead of a towel.
  6. Do not brush your hair when it is wet, it can cause breakage to it.
  7. About every 7 weeks, trim your hair one fourth of an inch to avoid the split ends.
  8. Using coconut, almond, castor, and olive oil for your hair is amazing.
  9. Smoking and eating unhealthy is bad for your hair.
  10. Clean out your brushes!!! Do not use the same ones forever. <<<<<<< Updated upstream =======
